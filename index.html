Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my data?

Your data is protected with end-to-end encryption using AES-256 encryption standards. We employ multiple layers of security including secure data centers, regular security audits, and two-factor authentication to ensure your files remain private and secure.

What storage plans do you offer?

We offer flexible plans starting from 5GB free storage for personal use. Premium plans include 100GB for $4.99/month, 1TB for $9.99/month, and unlimited storage for teams at $19.99/user/month. All plans include unlimited file sharing and 30-day version history.

Can I access my files offline?

Yes! Our desktop and mobile apps allow you to mark files and folders for offline access. These files will be automatically synced when you reconnect to the internet, ensuring you always have access to your important documents wherever you are.
